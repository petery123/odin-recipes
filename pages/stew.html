Tomato Stew

Description

Beef stew is one of the most popular and recognizable Nigerian stew. It is loaded with beef, tripe, and beef liver. The whole stew comes together in one pot. Since this stew is tomato-based, it is sometimes also referred to as tomato stew.

Steps

  1. Blend the tomato mixture
  2. Broil and braise the meat
  3. Prep the tomato paste
  4. Caramelize the onions and add tomato paste
  5. Add meat stock and spices
  6. Add meat of choice
