Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice traditionally consists of rice, cooking oil, tomatoes, onion, chilies, and often some combination of other ingredients such as tomato paste, bell pepper, garlic, ginger, thyme, curry powder, grains of selim, and bouillon cubes or stock; recipes vary from country to country and cook to cook.

Steps

  1. Wash the peppers, onion, and tomatoes
  2. Blend the chopped ingredients together
  3. Rince the rice in water until it runs clear
  4. Heat the vegetable oil over medium heat
  5. Chop 1 onion and saute it in oil for 4 minutes
  6. Cook the tomato paste for 5 minutes
  7. Add in the pepper puree and spices
  8. Add the rice, chicken stock, water, and salt
  9. Cover and cook the rice for 20 min
  10. Simmer the rice for an additional 10-20 min
